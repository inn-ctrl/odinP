My Mandazi Recipe

Ugali is a popular central and east african dish

Made from mostly maize, this dish have stood the test of time and boasts its compatibility with local vegetables for soup. Meat and fish is not a bad companion either.

Ingredients

  • Maize or ugali flour
  • Water
  • Green vegies
  • Fresh Meat(preferably)
  • Fresh Tomatatoes and paste
  • Onions
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Step 1:Boil water, add flour and stir until perfect dough is formed
  2. Step 2:On the side, cut and prepare all vegetables
  3. Step 3:Fry oil, and add onions when ready. gradually add all ingredients to make a soup
  4. Step 4:Boil meat, and fry it later.(do not add water). Instead add soup from step 3 and let boil
  5. Step 5:Play some Afro-congo music and enjoy the best meal in East and Central Africa!
